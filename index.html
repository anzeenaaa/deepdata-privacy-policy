This Privacy Policy applies to the DeepDATA mobile application, developed by ICAR-Central Marine Fisheries Research Institute (CMFRI), hereafter referred to as the “Service Provider”. By downloading, accessing, or using the application, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

The application may collect certain information when you use the service, including but not limited to your name, email address, uploaded species images and scientific contributions, device information such as operating system and device type, application usage statistics and logs, and Internet Protocol (IP) address. 2. How Information Is Used

Collected information may be used for providing and maintaining application functionality, user authentication and account management, managing scientific species contributions, improving application performance and user experience, responding to support requests and inquiries, and ensuring security and preventing unauthorized access. Users may upload species-related images, observations, and descriptive information through the application. By uploading content, users confirm that the uploaded material does not infringe copyright or ownership rights, the information provided is accurate to the best of their knowledge, and the uploaded content is lawful and appropriate. Uploaded content may be reviewed or moderated by the Service Provider before being publicly accessible within the application.

These applications operate under their own respective privacy policies and terms.

6. Sharing of Information

The Service Provider does not sell or rent personal information to third parties. Information may only be shared when required by applicable law or legal process, to protect the rights, safety, and security of users or the Service Provider, or with trusted service providers assisting in application operations.

User information and uploaded content may be retained for as long as necessary to provide application services, maintain scientific records, or comply with legal obligations. Users may request deletion of their account or personal data by contacting the Service Provider.
